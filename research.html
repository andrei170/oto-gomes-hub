6. Compliance Surface (regulated niche, A5)

Crypto + tax + income claims = doubly regulated. Public-facing surfaces require: - SEC scope: any "investment advice" framing triggers SEC consideration. OTO positions as educator, not advisor, which is the correct posture. - IRS scope: tax structuring guidance must be framed as education, not personalized tax advice; learners should consult licensed CPAs / tax attorneys. - FinCEN scope: discussion of trust structuring + crypto custody must avoid implying money-transmission or money-laundering avoidance. - FTC scope: any income claim ($20K → $17M, $500 → $10K, $5K-$10K mentorship outcome) needs a "results not typical, past performance not indicative" footer.

Landing page compliance footer (mandatory) includes: educational not personalized advice, crypto trading involves risk of total loss, past performance not indicative, OTO is an educator not a registered tax advisor or financial advisor, students should consult licensed professionals for personalized advice, FTC results-not-typical disclaimer.

7. Five Bottlenecks (depth audit, ranked by revenue impact)

Full bottleneck audit lives in bottleneck-analysis. One-paragraph summaries here.

B1 (Install) · Six Product Endpoints, No Visible Ladder. Six live URLs across one domain with inconsistent brand naming (CME / Circle / Academy / MasterHeart / 1:1 / Free Training). Cold visitor can't pick a starting point. The $497-$2K "missing middle" between CME ($997) and 1:1 ($5-10K) is the highest-leverage gap.

B2 (Install) · The Tax + Crypto Niche Moat Is Buried. OTO's sharpest positioning lives in the About page and podcast interviews. The homepage and IG bio lead with generic "Money Mentor and Crypto Coach" language. The 16-year-tax + 14-year-crypto overlap is structurally rare and not currently doing the cold-conversion work it should.

B3 (Install) · No Hero VSL Anywhere On The Funnel. Every sales page is text + static images. At $5K-$10K mentorship and $997 course price points, text-only sites convert cold traffic at 1-2%. A hero VSL on the homepage + per-tier cutdowns would lift cold conversion to 3-5%.

B4 (Install) · Podcast Catalogue + Guest Appearances Not Captured Into On-Site Nurture. The OTO Gomes Crypto Show plus guest spots on The Freedom People, NYC Podcast Network, The Way Forward, Conscious Initiatives all live on third-party platforms with no observable on-site capture. Warmest segment in the stack, currently unmonetized.

B5 (Recurring Engagement) · LinkedIn + YouTube Long-Form Distribution Underweight For The Tax + Crypto B2B Layer. OTO's tax-and-crypto angle is structurally LinkedIn-favoured (high-earning professionals, founders, family-office adjacents who screen-vet on LinkedIn before paying $5-10K). LinkedIn long-form cadence is underbuilt for that ICP layer. YouTube long-form is underweight relative to the podcast catalogue that already exists.
