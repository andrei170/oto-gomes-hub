Short preview script for the hero VSL on the consolidated homepage, plus production notes and per-tier cutdown briefs. The full VSL records to 4-7 minutes on Day 1 of the Install. This preview is the 90-second cold-traffic cut that lives on the AG1 hub-page poster + the landing-page poster.

Hero VSL · 4-7 min full version (production brief)

Open (0:00 to 0:30): Direct, anti-hype, moat-led.

Most crypto coaches teach one of three things. The trade, the tax, or the mindset. The reason there's a $5-10K tier of crypto coaching is that the operator with significant holdings needs all three at once.

I've been in tax accounting since I was 16, in crypto since 2013, and teaching the structural overlap between the two for the last six years. The framework I teach isn't the trade. It's the structure under the trade.

Founder arc beat (0:30 to 2:00): The $20K to $17M arc, told concisely with the structural lessons surfaced.

In 2008 I watched $120K disappear in the real estate crash. I was 22, I'd been in tax accounting for six years, I knew the structures that would have protected the position. I hadn't installed them.

In 2013 I bought my first crypto. By 2016 I'd been divorced, sold the house, walked away with $40K. Half of that went into crypto. The other half went into rebuilding.

Three years later that crypto position was around $17 million.

The structures I run now are the structures I should have had in 2008. The framework didn't come from the gains. It came from the loss.

Methodology beat (2:00 to 4:00): The seven structures, the trust layer, the sequence.

The framework runs on seven tax structures. Each one is publicly available. None of them is a secret. What's rare is the sequence.

Year one is the foundation. Personal entity, position sizing, the abundance and mindset work that lets you hold through a 70 percent drawdown without selling at the bottom.

Year two is the protection. The LLC layer, the asset separation, the first piece of structural insurance over the position.

Year three to five is the preservation. The trust mechanism. Private trust, common law structuring, the asset-protection and estate layer that separates the holder from the holdings.

Year five and beyond is the extension. Tax-aware exit timing, charitable structuring, the long-game layers.

Most holders run year-one defaults at year-five wealth levels. The framework runs the sequence in the right order.

Compliance + register beat (4:00 to 5:00): Educator, not advisor. Conscious-operator overlay.

I teach this framework. I'm not a registered tax advisor or financial advisor, and the framework isn't personalized advice. The framework is the operating layer; your own licensed professional is the execution layer.

The other thing the framework includes that most crypto curricula skip is the inner work. The mindset, the abundance frame, the healing-your-money-story layer. The structure protects the capital. The mindset is what keeps you in the position when the market is testing your conviction.

Offer + CTA beat (5:00 to 6:30): The three tiers.

There are three ways to install this:

If you're early-stage, year one to two, position under $50K, the Crypto Made Easy course is the foundation curriculum. Self-paced, public pricing.

If you're year two to four, position $50K to $250K, the Crypto Freedom Circle is the 10-week group mentorship. Application-only, cohort cadence.

If you're year three or beyond, position $250K+, the 1:1 Private Mentorship is the bespoke engagement. Application-only, full sequence.

The diagnostic on this page routes you to the tier that fits. Three questions, ninety seconds.

Close (6:30 to 7:00): Soft.

Whether or not you decide any of the tiers fit, the seven-structures guide is below. Download it. Run the audit on your own holdings. If you have questions, my IG DMs are open and the contact form on this page routes to my inbox.

Educational only, not personalized advice, but the framework is yours.
