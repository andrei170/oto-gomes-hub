Phase 3

Content Strategy · OTO Gomes

Distribution engine across IG (primary, already-active), LinkedIn (under-built, structural fit for tax-and-crypto B2B layer), and YouTube long-form (under-deployed, podcast catalogue is sunk-cost fuel). Prepared 2026-05-12 by AG1 Consulting.

The strategy is built around OTO's three audiences operating on three platforms with different intent:

  1. IG audience (104K, primary). Crypto-curious, abundance-and-freedom register, mixed early-stage and high-earning. Already trained on OTO's voice via Reels + Stories + IG Live.
  2. LinkedIn audience (under-built). High-earning professionals, founders, family-office adjacents, real-estate operators bridging into crypto. Screen-vet on credentials, pay $5-10K for niche-leader mentorship.
  3. YouTube + podcast audience (warmest segment). People who already invested 30+ minutes with OTO's voice; the most pre-qualified buyers in the stack. Currently uncaptured into nurture.

Three content pillars

Pillar 1 · Tax-Aware Crypto Structuring (the moat)

The pillar that re-positions OTO from "generic crypto coach" to "the rare tax-and-crypto operator." Owns the highest-intent keywords in the niche. Lives across all three platforms.

Topic spine: - The 7 tax structures crypto holders get wrong - Private trust + common law for crypto wealth: what it actually does - Capital gains tax timing on crypto exits - The difference between "crypto on a spreadsheet" tax filing and "crypto inside a structured trust" tax filing - Why the IRS treats crypto like property (and what that means in practice) - The cross-border tax exposure for US-resident crypto holders with LATAM connections - Tax-loss harvesting playbook for crypto portfolios

Surface deployment: - IG carousels (educational, dense): one tax-and-crypto carousel per week - IG reels (short-form, hook-led): 2-3x/week, single-claim format - LinkedIn long-form essays: 2x/week, 200-350 words, operator-to-operator register - YouTube long-form: 1x/week, 8-14 min, podcast-style breakdown with B-roll

Pillar 2 · The $20K to $17M Founder Arc (the proof)

The narrative pillar. The story is the cold-conversion lever. Every platform gets some surface of the arc.

Topic spine: - The 2008 real estate crash ($120K loss) → the discipline that came out of it - The 2016 divorce settlement → why $20K of the $40K house sale went into crypto - 2013 first crypto position → the early-cycle mistakes - The 2017 house fire → the actual lived experience of "lose everything and rebuild" - The California acting pivot → why it mattered for the operator he became - The $17M arc unpacked: which positions did the work, which positions didn't - What he'd do differently with $20K starting today

Surface deployment: - IG Reels (story-led, 60-90 sec): 1x/week - IG carousel (long-form story): 1x/month - LinkedIn essay: 1x/week, story-led + operator-lesson register - YouTube long-form: 1x/month, full arc breakdown with timeline - Podcast guest-appearance highlight reels repurposed into IG + YouTube Shorts

Pillar 3 · The Abundance + Consciousness Layer (the secondary register)

The pillar that anchors the existing audience and signals that OTO is the rare crypto operator who has done the inner work. Stays as the secondary register, not the primary moat.

Topic spine: - Healing your money story before you scale your crypto position - The "gamification of abundance" framing - Crypto as a sovereignty + freedom tool, not just a wealth-storage tool - The mindset work that lets a holder ride a 70% drawdown without selling - The emotional and psychological structures behind sustainable wealth - Why high-earners burn out before they hit the wealth threshold

Surface deployment: - IG Reels + IG Live: 1-2x/week (highest cadence on this pillar, where his existing audience expects it) - Podcast monologues + guest spots: ongoing - LinkedIn essays: 1x/month (lighter cadence on this surface, register-fit is softer) - YouTube long-form: 1x/month

Compliance + content guardrails (A5)

Every piece of content that touches an income claim, a specific tax structure, or a portfolio outcome carries paired language. Pulled from the canonical AG1 compliance pattern for the crypto + tax niche:

  • "Educational, not personalized tax or financial advice."
  • "Past performance is not indicative of future results."
  • "Consult a licensed tax professional or financial advisor before structuring any holdings."
  • "Crypto investing involves risk of substantial or total loss."
  • "Results are not typical."

These run as standing footers on every long-form surface (LinkedIn essay, YouTube description, podcast companion landing) and as inline disclaimer language on every piece that names a specific tax structure or trust mechanism. Full footer text lives on landing-page.html.

Measurement layer

  • IG: reach + saves + DMs as leading indicators. Workbook + moat-magnet opt-in volume as the conversion metric.
  • LinkedIn: impressions + profile views + connection requests from ICP-fit professionals. 1:1 inbound application rate as the conversion metric.
  • YouTube: watch time + subscriber growth + tax/crypto/trust query traffic as the search-discovery indicator. CME + Circle conversions sourced from YouTube as the revenue metric.
  • Podcast: episode downloads + companion-landing opt-in conversion rate. Tier-routed application rate from podcast capture as the deep metric.

All measurement plugs into the email nurture and the ladder analytics. Quarterly review cadence with OTO inside the Install + Recurring Engagement.
