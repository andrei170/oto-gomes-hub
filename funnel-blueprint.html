Phase map

Phase 1 · Consolidation + Moat Reposition (Days 1-3)

  • Domain audit + sitemap consolidation: /crypto-freedom-academy redirects to home, MasterHeart folds inside Circle on the public nav, six endpoints reduce to three tier pages + one umbrella ladder.
  • Brand colour audit + lock (per research Section 1).
  • Consolidated homepage live: hero VSL slot + ladder + 3-question diagnostic.
  • IG bio rewrite ships (moat-led).
  • Hero VSL records (4-7 min, Tax + Crypto + Trust Moat -> $20K to $17M Arc -> Methodology -> Ladder -> CTA).

Phase 2 · Ladder + Email Nurture (Days 4-7)

  • Tier pages live: CME (entry, public price), Circle (mid, application-priced as a band $1.5-$3K), 1:1 (high, application). Per-tier VSL cut-downs embedded.
  • Typeform application form built with branching logic (3 questions route to right tier).
  • Moat-specific lead magnet ("The 7 Tax Structures Crypto Holders Get Wrong") written + designed + opt-in landing built.
  • ConvertKit 10-touch nurture sequence written + activated, tier-routed CTAs.
  • Compliance footer language deployed on every public surface.
  • Stripe checkout for CME confirmed wired.

Phase 3 · Podcast + LinkedIn Compound (Days 8-30, ongoing)

  • Top 5 podcast guest appearance landing pages build + publish (companion 3-4 min pre-frame video + episode-specific opt-in each).
  • The OTO Gomes Crypto Show episode-landing template ships; future episodes deploy with companions.
  • LinkedIn long-form cadence ramps to 4-5x/week (per content-strategy).
  • YouTube long-form repurpose of podcast catalogue starts 1-2x/week.
  • First quarterly Circle cohort opens under the new ladder.
  • IG pillar mix shifts to 50% Pillar 1 (tax + crypto moat) / 25% Pillar 2 (founder arc) / 25% Pillar 3 (abundance overlay).

Phase 4 · Retargeting + Scale (Days 31-90, ongoing)

  • Meta pixel + LinkedIn insight tag installed and firing.
  • Custom audiences built: IG engagement + site visitors + lead-magnet opt-ins + tier-page bouncers.
  • 3-ad retargeting sequence: moat ad -> founder-arc ad -> ladder-CTA ad.
  • LinkedIn cadence locks at 4-5x/week; first $5-10K 1:1 inbound from LinkedIn measured.
  • YouTube cadence to 2x/week; chapter markers and tax + crypto + trust queries reviewed for SEO compound.
  • Quarterly cohort cadence settles; mid-tier (Circle) pricing band confirms based on Days 7-90 conversion data.

Risk register

Risk Likelihood Mitigation
Crypto regulation tightens (SEC / IRS / FinCEN) during Install window Medium Compliance footer + educational-not-advice positioning carry the brand through; pivot any tax-structuring language to softer educational framing if needed
OTO's $20K to $17M claim flagged by FTC / state AG as unsubstantiated Low-medium Always paired with "results not typical, past performance not indicative" inline; never used as an outcome promise to buyers; stays anchored to founder narrative
Moat-specific lead magnet ("7 Tax Structures") attracts CPA / tax-attorney audit Low Built as education, not advice; named structures are publicly available concepts (private trust, common law, capital gains timing), not proprietary loopholes
Circle mid-tier pricing band reveals on public surface and cannibalises 1:1 Medium Application gate + intake conversation routes high-fit buyers up the ladder; band is range, not single number
ConvertKit list grows fast and triggers compliance complaint volume Low Compliance footer in every email + clear unsubscribe + explicit consent at opt-in
Hero VSL records badly or off-brand Low-medium Day-1 placeholder static poster; bespoke re-shoot Days 14-21; same brand colour treatment carries the poster
LinkedIn audience doesn't engage (under-built channel risk) Medium Cadence ramps gradually Day 8-30; if engagement is flat at Day 60, pivot the recurring engagement to YouTube-primary instead
Brazilian / Portuguese-speaking audience segment under-served Medium Optional Portuguese DM version included in outreach.md; if Portuguese capture exceeds 10% of IG inbound, build a /pt/ subpath for the moat-specific lead magnet at Month 3
Tax + crypto regulatory environment changes specific structures (trust, common law) Medium All curriculum framed as "current state, subject to change, consult licensed professional"; OTO's voice already carries this register; copy reinforces it

Notes

  • Install is built around the 104K IG audience as the primary distribution surface. LinkedIn and YouTube are layered for compound, not as front-door replacement.
  • The moat-specific lead magnet is the highest-leverage new asset in the build because it converts the high-intent buyer (the high-earning crypto holder Googling "crypto tax structure private trust") at significantly higher rates than the generic workbook.
  • The Crypto Freedom Circle middle tier is the structural beneficiary of the Install. The $497-$2K gap between CME and 1:1 is closed by surfacing Circle on the ladder with a visible pricing band, not just on an application form.
  • The conscious-operator secondary register stays alive. The brand doesn't lose the abundance / freedom / sovereignty audience it already has. The moat moves to the front; the register stays.
