Research Snapshot

5 bottleneck gaps identified

The Five Gaps

  • No VSL anywhere in the funnel. Every sales page is text-only. At $5K-$10K price points, a 3-5 minute VSL on the coaching page alone could double application volume.
  • Six product endpoints, no clear ladder. Crypto Made Easy, Freedom Circle, Freedom Academy, crypto-coaching, free-training, MasterHeart. New visitors cannot tell which one to enter or how they connect.
  • Tax-plus-crypto angle buried. 16 years tax + 14 years crypto is the single strongest differentiator. Lives in podcast interviews, not on homepage or IG bio.
  • Podcast appearances not embedded on-site. Freedom People, NYC Podcast Network, The Way Forward, own Spotify show. Strongest trust signals, all off-domain.
  • No visible email nurture after lead magnet. Workbook captures name and email, then nothing. At $5K-$10K price points, trust requires more than one touchpoint.

Highest-Impact Fix

Build a short VSL for the coaching application page. Reposition homepage around tax-aware crypto structuring. Consolidate six endpoints into one offer ladder with price hinting. Install a 10-email nurture from workbook opt-in to application.
