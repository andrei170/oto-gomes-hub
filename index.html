Six Product Endpoints, No Visible Ladder Between Them

Six live product URLs on otogomes.live with inconsistent brand naming. Crypto Made Easy ($997), Crypto Freedom Circle (application-only, price opaque), Private 1:1 Mentorship ($5K-$10K), MasterHeart (upsell), Free Training, the Crypto Freedom Academy umbrella name. Cold visitor cannot tell which to enter or how they relate. The $497-$2K middle tier between CME and the 1:1 is structurally invisible even though Crypto Freedom Circle is positioned to fill that exact gap.

Cost of Waiting

~$4-12K/mo in mid-tier Circle seats not converting because the ladder is invisible. ~$50K-$140K Year-1 floor on offer-ladder consolidation alone. The entry-tier reader assumes everything sits in the same range and bounces, while the high-tier prospect can't tell which container is the 1:1 vs MasterHeart vs Circle.

6 months from now.~$25K-$70K in mid-tier seats sold to competitors with simpler ladders. Crypto Freedom Circle keeps under-converting because it sits behind an application gate without a visible price band.

Fix. Visible 3-tier ladder under one umbrella. Entry tier (CME $497-$997, public price). Mid tier (Circle $1,500-$2,997 application-priced band). High tier (1:1 $5K-$10K application-only). MasterHeart folds inside Circle as an upsell layer. 3-question diagnostic routes prospects to the right tier.

The Tax + Crypto Niche Moat Is Buried In The About Page

OTO's sharpest differentiator: 16 years tax accounting (family business since age 16) + 14 years crypto operating (since 2013) + active education on private trust and common law structuring. That triple-overlap is structurally rare. Most crypto educators are day-traders without tax operating chops, or tax accountants who only recently added crypto. OTO sits at the intersection. The moat currently lives in About-page paragraph six and in long-form podcast interviews. The homepage and IG bio lead with generic "Money Mentor and Crypto Coach" language.

Cost of Waiting

~$3.5-10K/mo in premium-tier conversion drift. The 1:1 $5-10K buyer needs the tax + trust + structuring differentiator to justify the spend; without it on the homepage and coaching page, the 1:1 competes on generic-coach pricing, not on niche-leader pricing. ~$40K-$120K Year-1 floor on the moat repositioning.

6 months from now.~$20K-$60K in premium-tier inbound walking past. Family-office adjacents and significant-holding founders see "Money Mentor" and assume he's the abundance-only version, not the structuring-savvy version.

Fix. Reposition homepage hero and IG bio to lead with the tax + crypto + trust moat. Keep the abundance + consciousness register as the secondary layer. Coaching page hero opens on the moat, not on the freedom story. Hero VSL builds beats around the moat first, arc second.

Text is the wrong format for the audience he's already trained.

Cost of Waiting

~$5-13K/mo in cold-traffic conversion drift. Text-only sales pages convert cold traffic at 1-2%. With a hero VSL on the homepage and per-tier cutdowns, the floor moves to 3-5%. ~$60K-$160K Year-1 floor on the VSL install across the consolidated homepage and three tier landings.

6 months from now.~$30K-$80K never collected from the same traffic. The $20K to $17M founder arc is doing 10% of the work it could do because it lives in About-page text instead of as the first thing a cold prospect hears in OTO's voice.

Fix. Hero VSL (4-7 min) on the consolidated homepage. Beats. Tax + crypto + trust moat. $20K to $17M founder arc. Methodology overview. Offer ladder. Tier-routed CTA. Plus 2-3 min VSLs on each tier landing (CME, Circle, 1:1). Recorded once per surface, lives forever.

LinkedIn + YouTube Long-Form Underweight For The Tax + Crypto B2B Layer

OTO's strongest channel today is Instagram, but his 1:1 $5-10K ICP (high-earning professionals, founders, family-office adjacents, real-estate operators bridging into crypto) is structurally LinkedIn-favoured. Those buyers screen-vet on LinkedIn before paying premium for niche-leader crypto coaching. LinkedIn long-form cadence is under-built; YouTube long-form is underweight relative to the podcast catalogue that already exists as sunk-cost fuel.

Cost of Waiting

~$5-17K/mo in premium 1:1 inbound walking past on the LinkedIn feed. A single 1:1 mentorship engagement is worth $5K-$10K. ~$60K-$200K Year-1 floor on a monthly ongoing engagement covering LinkedIn 4-5x/week plus YouTube long-form 1-2x/week.

6 months from now.Another six months of competing crypto educators without the tax + trust + structuring credentials out-publishing on LinkedIn. The gap compounds. The podcast catalogue stays audio-only while audiences who'd buy live on YouTube.

Fix. Monthly ongoing engagement on LinkedIn long-form ghostwriting (4-5x/week, tax + crypto + trust register in OTO's voice) + YouTube long-form repurpose of the podcast catalogue (1-2x/week, with chapter markers for tax + crypto + trust queries). Bundled with the Install side of the engagement.
